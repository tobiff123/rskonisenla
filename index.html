About me

Dr. Oriolowo Aderemi Adesola, (Doctor Onisenla)

Dr. Oriolowo Aderemi Adesola, also known as Doctor Onisenla, is a highly qualified herbal practitioner with a BSc, MSc, and PhD in Botany. He is the founder of Onisenla Natural Herbal Therapy & Spiritual Consultancy, where he specializes in treating a variety of natural and spiritual ailments.

  • Dr. Aderemi is a bona fide member of the Association of Indigenous Holistic Herbal Practitioners and the National Association of Nigerian Traditional Medicine Practitioners (NANTMP). He is registered with the Lagos State Traditional Medicine Board (2024), reinforcing his commitment to professional standards and ethical practices in holistic healing.

  • With a passion for combining traditional herbal remedies and spiritual guidance, Dr. Aderemi strives to promote overall wellness and balance in the lives of his clients.
